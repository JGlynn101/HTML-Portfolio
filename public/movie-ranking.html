Joshua's Favorite Movies in the Whole Wide World!

Top 3 Movies

Arrival

This movie is perfect. The acting, starring Amy Adams, the plot, the tension it builds and both the reality and breadth of the characters make this movie a favorite of mine.

Coach Carter

I saw this movie as a 6 or 7 year old and loved it. It is a comfort movie I come back to occasionally when I need motivation.

Dan In Real Life

This movie is a Rom-Com and showcases my favorite actor's (Steve Carrell) versatility and range as an actor.
